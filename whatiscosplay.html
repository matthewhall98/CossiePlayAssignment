What is cosplay?

Costume play, or ‘Cosplay’ for short, is a performance art revolving around cosplayers (people) wearing various costumes and fashion accessories to inherit the appearance of a specific character. Cosplay is essentially any form costumed roleplay that is conducted in venues other than on a stage. Any entity that lends itself to dramatic interpretation may be taken up as a subject for cosplay. Gender switching happens to be very prominent in Cosplay. Favorite cosplay sources include manga, anime, comic books, cartoons, video games, live-action films and television series.

The rapid growth in the number of people cosplaying as a hobby since 1990 has made the phenomenon a significant aspect of popular culture in Japan and some other parts of Asia and in the Western world. Cosplay events are common features of fan conventions and there are also dedicated conventions, local and international competitions, as well as social networks, websites and other forms of media centered on cosplay activities.
- Paraphrased from Wikipedia
